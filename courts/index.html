Bankruptcy Courts by District

All 85 federal bankruptcy districts in our database, ranked by case volume. Linked districts have detailed filing guides.

Which Court Do I File In?

File in the federal district where you have lived (or where your principal place of business is located) for the greater part of the last 180 days.
