The Northern District of Indiana bankruptcy court serves South Bend/Fort Wayne, IN and surrounding areas.

Court website: www.innb.uscourts.gov

How to File

  1. Complete credit counseling. Take an approved course within 180 days before filing. Provider list at justice.gov.
  2. Choose your chapter. Chapter 7 (liquidation), Chapter 13 (repayment), or Subchapter V (small business).
  3. Complete official forms. Download from uscourts.gov. Required: Petition, Schedules A/B-J, SOFA, means test.
  4. File electronically or in person. Attorneys use CM/ECF. Pro se filers may file in person.
  5. Pay the filing fee. Ch.7: $338. Ch.13: $313. Fee waivers for Ch.7 below 150% poverty. Installments available.
  6. Attend 341 Meeting of Creditors. Scheduled 20-40 days after filing. Telephone/video often available.
  7. Complete financial management course. Required before discharge.
  8. Receive discharge. Ch.7: 60-90 days after 341. Ch.13: after completing 3-5 year plan.
